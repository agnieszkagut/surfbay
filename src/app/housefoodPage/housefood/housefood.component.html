Surf house

Welcome to a place you can call home. Our surf house has a combination of moroccan traditional and modern styles, which make you feel like your own Moroccan home

Our surf guesthouse offers you flats that host up to 6 people, with shared bathrooms, living rooms, and a kitchenette. For couples, families or those who look for more privacy, we offer you private apartments with private bathrooms, living rooms, and a kitchenette

We have apartments with small private terraces or small balconies, which makes you feel at home with a new family - the surf family

You can connect directly with the sun, read your book and enjoy the atmosphere on our rooftop with the ocean view

Food

We offer meals throughout the day (breakfast, lunch, snacks, dinner and filtered water. All meals are cooked and presented as Moroccan cuisine with fresh and rich ingredients to provide your body with the energy needed to surf, considering quality and hygiene
