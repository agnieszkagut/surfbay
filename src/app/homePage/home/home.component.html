We are located in the south of MOROCCO, 14 km from AGADIR, in TAMRAGHT village, just next to the beautiful TAGHAZOUT. Some of the southern surf spots are just minutes away from your doorstep, such as Devil’s rock, CroCro beach, and Banana point.

In addition to surfing, we also offer a variety of activities to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. You will be able to participate in YOGA classes, day trip to paradise valley, visiting the big traditional market (Souk Alhed), and sandboarding in the sand dunes, we will even have an escape to the middle of the ocean with SUPs, rolling with bicycles by the beachside. As well as immerse yourself in the local scene and through the local streets, shop at outdoor markets, and taste the traditional cuisine.
